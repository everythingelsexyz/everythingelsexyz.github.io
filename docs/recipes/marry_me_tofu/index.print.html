Two blocks super firm tofu

2T Vegan butter 5 Cloves minced garlic 1T Italian seasoning 1t calabrian chiles (or red pepper flakes) 2C vegan chick’n broth 1T Dijon mustard (Edmond Fallot preferred over grey poupon)

1/2C Vegan follow your heart parmesan 1/3C Sun dried tomatoes Salt and pepper to taste

Fettuccine
